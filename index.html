My Favourite Hobby: Photography

Photography has always been my passion. Capturing moments and turning them into memories gives me immense joy. I love exploring different types of photography, from landscapes to portraits and street photography. Every picture tells a story, and with the right angle, light, and composition, you can express emotions that words sometimes cannot. Photography has also taught me patience and observation. Whether using a smartphone or a professional camera, the key is to see beauty in everyday life and preserve it forever. Sharing photos with friends makes it even more rewarding, especially when i get compliments from my work.

Types of Photography I Enjoy:

  • Landscape Photography
  • Portrait Photography
  • Street Photography
  • Macro Photography
